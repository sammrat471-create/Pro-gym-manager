What is Fitness?

Fitness is the ability of your body to perform daily physical activities efficiently without feeling tired. It includes strength, endurance, flexibility, and mental well-being. Regular exercise improves heart health, increases stamina, and helps maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Why Fitness is Important?

Maintaining fitness helps prevent diseases such as obesity, diabetes, and heart problems. It improves mood, reduces stress, and boosts confidence. A fit body leads to a longer and healthier life.
