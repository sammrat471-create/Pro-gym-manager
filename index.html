What is Fitness?

Fitness is the ability of your body to perform daily activities efficiently. It includes strength, endurance, flexibility, and mental health. Regular exercise improves heart health, boosts stamina, and helps maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Why is Fitness Important?

Maintaining fitness reduces the risk of diseases like obesity, diabetes, and heart problems. It also improves mood, reduces stress, and increases confidence. A fit lifestyle leads to a longer and healthier life.
