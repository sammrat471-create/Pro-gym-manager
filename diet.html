Complete Diet Plan for Fitness

A proper diet is essential for achieving your fitness goals. Protein is important for muscle growth and repair. Sources include eggs, chicken, fish, and legumes.

Carbohydrates provide energy for workouts. Include rice, oats, and whole grains. Healthy fats such as nuts and olive oil are also important for hormone balance.

Drink at least 2–3 liters of water daily. Avoid junk food and sugary drinks. Eating balanced meals at the right time helps improve performance and recovery.
