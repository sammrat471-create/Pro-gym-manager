Complete Diet Guide for Fitness

A proper diet plays a crucial role in achieving fitness goals. Whether you want to build muscle, lose weight, or stay healthy, your nutrition determines your results.

1. Importance of Protein

Protein helps in muscle growth and repair. It is essential for people who do regular workouts. Common protein sources include eggs, chicken, fish, milk, paneer, and legumes.

2. Role of Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates provide energy for daily activities and workouts. Include complex carbs like rice, oats, and whole grains instead of junk food.

3. Healthy Fats

Healthy fats are important for hormone balance and overall health. Include nuts, seeds, and olive oil in your diet.

4. Hydration

Drinking enough water is very important. It helps in digestion, improves performance, and keeps the body active.

5. Balanced Diet Plan

A balanced diet should include protein, carbs, fats, vitamins, and minerals. Avoid processed foods and focus on natural foods.
