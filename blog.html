Complete Workout Plan for Beginners

Starting your fitness journey requires consistency, patience, and a proper workout plan. Beginners should focus on compound exercises like push-ups, squats, lunges, and planks. These exercises target multiple muscle groups and build a strong foundation.

Training should be done 3 to 4 times per week. Each session should include warm-up exercises, main workout, and cool-down stretching. A proper warm-up increases blood flow and reduces the risk of injury.

It is important to gradually increase intensity. Start with light weights and focus on proper form. As your strength improves, you can increase weight and repetitions.

Rest and recovery are equally important. Muscles grow when you rest, not when you train. Ensure you get at least 7–8 hours of sleep daily.
