Best Workout Plan for Beginners

Beginners should focus on basic exercises like push-ups, squats, and planks. Training 3–4 times a week is ideal. Always start with warm-up and end with stretching to avoid injuries.

How to Lose Weight Effectively

Weight loss requires a calorie deficit. Combine regular exercise with a healthy diet. Avoid junk food and stay consistent with your routine.

Muscle Building Tips

To build muscle, focus on strength training and consume enough protein. Rest and recovery are equally important for muscle growth.
