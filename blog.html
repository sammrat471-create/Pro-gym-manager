Best Workout Plan for Beginners

Starting a fitness journey can be challenging, but a proper workout plan makes it easier. Beginners should focus on full-body workouts that include exercises like push-ups, squats, and planks.

Training 3–4 times per week is ideal. Always start with warm-up exercises to prevent injuries. Gradually increase intensity as your body adapts.

Consistency is key. Even simple workouts done regularly can give excellent results over time.
